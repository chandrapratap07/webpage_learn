About Me

I am a researcher at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, which is one of the premier institutes in India. My area of research includes development and testing of algorithms for multiphase flows and high-resolution methods in the field of Computational Fluid Dynamics. I am a programming enthusiast and I program in many languages like Java, C/C++, Python, Maple, HTML, javascript etc. In my free time, I upload videos related to programming of computational fluid dynamics software on my YouTube® channel.
